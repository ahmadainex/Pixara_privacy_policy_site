Privacy Policy

Pixara - Gallery & Organizer

Introduction

Thank you for choosing Pixara. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

The bottom line: Pixara works entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or share your personal photos or data.

Data Collection & Usage

We do not collect any personal data. All processing of your images and albums happens locally on your device.

  • Photos & Videos: Your media files remain on your device storage. The app only accesses them to display and organize them as per your commands.
  • No Cloud Uploads: Pixara does not upload your content to any external servers or cloud storage.
  • No Analytics: We do not track your usage behavior or collect identifiers.

Permissions We Request

To function as a gallery, Pixara requires specific permissions on your device:

  • Read/Write External Storage (or Photos/Media): Required to display your photos, create albums, and save changes (like deletions or moves).
  • Camera: Required only if you choose to take a photo directly from within the app.

Security

Since your data never leaves your device, it is as secure as your device itself. We rely on your device's operating system security features to protect your local files.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Revised" date and will be effective as soon as it is accessible.
